INTRODUCTION

Central Rescue Crusaders (CRC) is a body of Christian students and associates from Nyandarua, Nyeri, Kirinyaga, Murang’a, Kiambu, Laikipia and Nairobi counties bound together by a common faith in the only God and joint together with the heavenly calling of Christ according to the Holy scriptures and IT DECLARES that it shall be non-political, non-denominational, voluntal and non-partisan AND FURTHER DECLARES that no provision therein shall or will be construed as to qualify defeat or in any manner contravene the Holy Scriptures and such provision shall to the extent of contravention have no effect WITHOUT PREJUDICE to the foregoing


CRC-MUCU MC is an evangelistic team registered under moi university christian union main campus MUCU MC CRC is recognized by Moi University Christian Union Main Campus (M.U.C.U-M.C) as an Evangelistic Team and all the CRC’s interests shall be subject to those of the M.U.C.U-M.C. further we recognize MUCU as our mother church and always look forward to see the church grow both in prayer and the word.

AIMS OF CENTRAL RESCUE CRUSADERS- MUCU MC


The aims of CRC shall be non-political, non-denominational and shall be as follows:
i. To deepen and strengthen the spiritual life of its members by the study of the Bible, by prayer and by Christian fellowship.
ii. To witness the Lord Jesus Christ as God incarnate in Nyandarua, Nyeri, Kirinyaga, Murang’a, Kiambu, Nairobi and Laikipia counties.
iii. To encourage social interaction and discipleship among the members of CRC.
iv. To make contact with other CRC fellowships in other campuses and also with other Christian fellowships.
v. To maintain a close contact with the CRC associates

CRC DOCTRINAL BASIS

The doctrinal basis of CRC shall be the fundamental TRUTH of Christianity including:
  1. The unity of the Father, The Son and the Holy Spirit in the God head
  2. The doctrinal basis of CRC shall be the fundamental TRUTH of Christianity including
  3. The divine inspiration and entire truthfulness of the Holy Scriptures originally given and its supreme authority in all matters of faith and conduct.
  4. The universal sinfulness and guilt of all men since the fall rendering them subject to God’s wrath and condemnation.
  5. Redemption from the guilt, penalty, dominion and pollution of sin SOLELY through the sacrificial death of the Lord Jesus Christ, the incarnate Son of God as our representative and substitute.
  6. Justification of a sinner by the Grace of God through Faith alone
  7. The bodily resurrection of the Lord Jesus Christ from the dead and His ascension to the right hand of God through Faith alone.
  8. The presence and power of the Holy Spirit in the work of regeneration.
  9. The indwelling and work of the Holy Spirit in every believer.
  10. The one holy universal church is the body of Christ and to which all true believers in unity belong.
  11. The expectation of the personal return of the Lord Jesus Christ and the subsequent eternal life of the holy universal church in heaven with Him.
